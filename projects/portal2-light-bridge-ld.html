Key Design Goals

- Explore Lightbridge Mechanics: The entire level centers around this single mechanic, allowing players to progressively discover and master its various applications.

- Progressive Challenge Design: Puzzles introduce concepts in logical sequence with gradually increasing complexity, encouraging both object manipulation and creative player interaction with physics.
